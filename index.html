Kline Cocktail Compendium

Why Six Cocktails?

Death & Company, an iconic cocktail bar in New York that was on the forefront of the craft cocktail revolution since opening day, 12/31/06, focuses on both classic and new, innovative beverages. In their second book, Cocktail Codex, the founders (Alex Day, Nick Fauchald, and David Kaplan) propose that most cocktails are variations of six classic, or root cocktails. This approach is followed in the Kline Cocktail Compendium. The recipes are from many sources.
